About MindTrackSU

Designed to help you track, understand, and respond to how you are feeling.

MindTrackSU is an anonymous companion for mental wellness. It helps you log patterns, uncover mood trends, and access care resources without sharing personal details.

  • Anonymous by design. Your responses stay private and secure.
  • Clear personal insight. Simple mood check-ins and trend summaries help you see progress.
  • Support when you need it. Find trusted resources, emergency guidance, and next steps.
